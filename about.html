Welcome!

My name is Eli Jordan, I grew up in rural Tennessee before starting my education at the University of Mississippi. After 2 undergraduate years wandering through a Mechanical Engineering path, I decided to transfer to CU Boulder to study Creative Technology and Design and Computer Science in the ATLAS Institute. Since then, I have developed computer skills I never dreamed of, developed a passion for beautiful program design and a care for the algorithms that drive them. My current goal is developing web3 and building a decentralized internet. I believe the internet should be controlled by the people that use it.

My favorite project so far is the Kraken Automated Trading Application. An algorithmic trading bot I designed to trade cryptocurrencies using the Kraken Exchange API.
